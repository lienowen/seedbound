# Pocket Planet V0.2

## Added

- Five-mission ecology map with progressive unlocking.
- Four objective types: growth, water restoration, fire control, and crystal creation.
- Shared daily-seed challenge.
- Undo that restores the full previous simulation state.
- Persistent XP, completed missions, unlocked missions, and discoveries.
- Contextual mission hints and reaction guide.
- Expanded reaction rules for water, seed, fire, ice, and wind.
- Mission result flow with next mission, retry, map, and share actions.

## Verified

- Production build passes.
- Mission map opens and locked missions cannot be selected.
- First Light wins with `Seed -> Water -> Water` at one target.
- Completing First Light unlocks The Thaw and awards XP.
- Undo restores the world and action count in The Thaw.
- Result panels expose the correct next, retry, map, and share actions.
