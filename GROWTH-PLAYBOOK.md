# Pocket Planet Growth Playbook

## How It Plays

The player notices movement in the paper valley, taps a region, and enters a
close-up ecology scene. They use a small set of natural elements to solve the
local problem. The solution permanently changes the main world and may reveal a
hidden species.

Core loop:

1. Notice a curious detail.
2. Enter the region.
3. Experiment with two to five actions.
4. Watch a satisfying ecological chain.
5. Discover a species or secret.
6. Return to a visibly changed valley.
7. Inspect another region or return tomorrow.

The game should create the question: "What is happening inside that place?"

## Acquisition

### Short Video

Use real gameplay rather than fake rescue ads.

- Hook: "There is something moving inside this paper forest."
- Hook: "Only three drops can wake this valley."
- Hook: "Everyone gets the same ecosystem today. What would you do first?"
- Format: curiosity at 0-2 seconds, interaction at 2-5 seconds, chain reaction
  at 5-9 seconds, hidden reveal at 9-12 seconds.
- CTA: "Open the same valley and try your solution."

Channels: TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, playable ad networks, and
creator posts in cozy-game, puzzle, craft, and nature communities.

### Organic Search

Build playable landing pages around outcomes rather than generic genre terms:

- relaxing ecosystem puzzle
- paper craft nature game
- daily three move puzzle
- cozy browser game no download
- build a tiny living world

### Creator Seeding

Give small creators a unique challenge seed. Their audience should open the
exact same ecosystem without registration. Pay for completed-play referrals,
not impressions.

## Viral Loop

The share unit is an eight-second before-and-after ecology replay, not a score
card.

1. Player solves or discovers something.
2. The game creates a short replay that hides the exact input order.
3. The link says: "Can you wake this same valley?"
4. Friend opens directly into the same seed.
5. Results are compared only after both finish.
6. Both unlock a small shared discovery when the friend completes.

Avoid rewards based on sending spam. Reward successful shared play.

## Metrics

Core:

- First interaction time
- Region detail open rate
- Puzzle completion
- Immediate retry
- Second region opened
- Session length
- D1 and D7 retention

Growth:

- Replay creation rate
- Share rate
- Share click-through
- Invite landing-to-play conversion
- Invited player completion and D1
- K-factor based on completed invited plays

## Monetization

Do not monetize before retention works.

Suitable later:

- Cosmetic biome packs
- Seasonal paper-craft worlds
- Optional personal-world expansion
- One-time premium edition

Avoid:

- Selling puzzle solutions
- Energy timers
- Forced interstitial ads
- Pay-to-win items
